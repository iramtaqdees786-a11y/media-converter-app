### **3. Aggressive SEO Features**

#### **On-Page Optimization**:
- ✅ CTR-optimized title tags (50-60 chars with modifiers)
- ✅ Action-oriented meta descriptions (140-155 chars with CTAs)
- ✅ Primary keyword in H1
- ✅ Logical heading hierarchy (H2/H3)

#### **Structured Data** (Schema.org):
- ✅ **SoftwareApplication Schema** - For app rich results
- ✅ **FAQ Schema** - For "People Also Ask" rich results
- ✅ **HowTo Schema** - For step-by-step rich results
- ✅ **WebSite Schema** - For sitelinks search box
- ✅ **Organization Schema** - For knowledge panel

#### **Content Strategy**:
- ✅ How-to guides with step-by-step instructions
- ✅ Benefits sections highlighting USPs
- ✅ FAQ sections (5-8 questions per page)
- ✅ Comparison tables for increased dwell time
- ✅ Related tools for internal linking

### **4. Internal Linking Architecture**

**Topic Clusters**:
- **Image Converters**: HEIC→JPG, PNG→JPG, WebP→JPG, etc.
- **PDF Tools**: PDF→Word, PDF→Excel, PDF Compress, etc.
- **Video Tools**: MP4→MP3, Video Downloader, Video Trimmer
- **Audio Tools**: MP3 converters, audio extractors

**Linking Strategy**:
- Each page links to 6 related tools
- Hub pages link to all category tools
- Homepage links to popular tools
- Footer links to all major pages

### **5. Automation Scripts**

#### **Page Generator** (`generate_seo_pages.py`):
- Programmatically generates SEO-optimized pages
- Includes structured data, FAQs, related tools
- Scalable to 100+ pages
- **Generated**: 5 additional converter pages

#### **Sitemap Generator** (`generate_sitemap.py`):
- Auto-detects HTML pages
- Assigns priorities based on importance
- Generates XML sitemap (72 pages)
- Creates robots.txt
- **Ready for Google Search Console**
