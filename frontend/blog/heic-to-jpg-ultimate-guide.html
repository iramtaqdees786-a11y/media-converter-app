If you use an iPhone, you've likely encountered HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) files. While they save space, they often cause compatibility issues when shared with Windows users or uploaded to websites. In this guide, we'll master the art of conversion.

Why Convert HEIC to JPG?

HEIC offers superior compression, but JPG remains the universal standard for digital images. Converting ensures your photos can be viewed on any device, from old PCs to smart TVs.
