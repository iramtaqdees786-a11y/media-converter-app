The Zero-Persistence Laboratory Standard

At ConvertRocket, we believe in Zero-Persistence Architecture. Unlike legacy cloud converters that store copies of your documents on persistent hard drives for "caching" or "debugging," our Neural Engine operates entirely within volatile memory pools (RAM). Once your conversion session terminates, the data is instantly vaporized from our registers. This ensures that even in the event of a hypothetical server breach, there is no data at rest to be compromised.

Technical Specification: RAM-Only Buffer

Our backend utilizes isolated tmpfs volumes. These are ramdisk partitions that physically cannot retain data after a process restart or 60-minute TTL expiry. Your files never touch a magnetic platter or flash cell during the conversion protocol.

End-to-End Encryption & Secure Handshakes

Every kilobyte of data inducted into our laboratory is protected by military-grade SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encryption during transit. This creates a secure tunnel between your browser and our processing units, preventing man-in-the-middle attacks. Furthermore, our modern WebAssembly (WASM) modules allow some tools to run entirely client-side, meaning your raw data never even leaves your local machine—the ultimate form of digital privacy.
