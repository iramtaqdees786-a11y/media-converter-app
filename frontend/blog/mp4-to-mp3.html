Have a video lecture, a concert recording, or a podcast episode in video format that you want to listen to on the go? Converting MP4 to MP3 is the solution. MP3 files are much smaller and are supported by every audio player in the world.

The Process

With ConvertRocket's specialized MP4 to MP3 tool, extracting audio is effortless. You upload your video file, and our server strips the video track, leaving you with a crystal-clear audio file. We support high bitrates (up to 320kbps) ensuring no loss in sound quality.

Why Choose MP3 Over M4A or AAC?

While AAC offers slightly better quality at lower bitrates, MP3 is the king of compatibility. Some older car stereos or specialized equipment might not play AAC files, but they will definitely play MP3s.
