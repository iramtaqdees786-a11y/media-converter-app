The Challenge with PDFs

PDF (Portable Document Format) strictly defines where characters are placed on a page visually, but it doesn't "know" what a row or column is. To a computer, a table in a PDF just looks like floating words.

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) combined with layout analysis algorithms are needed to "see" the table structure. ConvertRocket uses advanced algorithms to detect grid lines and alignment to reconstruct the table purely in Excel format.

Best Practices for Scanning

To get the best results when converting scanned paper documents:

  • Resolution: Scan at least at 300 DPI. Blurry text is hard for AI to read.
  • Straightness: Ensure the paper is straight. Skewed tables are difficult to process.
  • Contrast: Black text on white paper works best. Avoid colored backgrounds if possible.
