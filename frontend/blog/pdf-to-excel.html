PDF to Excel: Retain Formulas and Formatting

Published on Dec 19, 2025 • Productivity

Data trapped in PDF files is useless. You can't sort it, filter it, or run formulas on it. That's why converting PDF to Excel is a critical workflow for accountants, data analysts, and researchers.

How It Works

ConvertRocket uses advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and layout analysis to identify tables within your PDF document. It then reconstructs these tables in an Excel (.xlsx) file, preserving rows and columns accurately.

Best Practices for PDF Conversion

  • Clear Scans: If your PDF is a scan, ensure it's high resolution.
  • Standard Fonts: Standard business fonts convert more accurately than handwriting.
