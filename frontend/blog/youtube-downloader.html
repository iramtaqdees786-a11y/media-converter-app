Choosing the Right Format: MP4 vs. WebM

When downloading, you might see options for MP4 and WebM. Which one should you choose?

  • MP4 (H.264): The most compatible format. Works on iPhone, Android, Windows, Mac, and TVs. Choose this if you want to share the file.
  • WebM (VP9): Google's open format. Often provides better quality at smaller file sizes, but might not play on older iPhones without a third-party player (like VLC).

Safety Tips for 2025: Avoiding Malware

The internet is full of "free downloaders" that are essentially traps. Here is how to spot a fake or dangerous site:

  • Excessive Pop-ups: If you click "Download" and 3 new windows open, leave immediately. ConvertRocket has a strict no-invasive-ads policy.
  • "Update Your Player" Scam: Never click on a popup that says your Flash Player or Video Player is outdated. This is 100% always malware.
  • exe Files: If you are trying to download a video and the file ends in .exe or .msi, DELETE IT. Video files should end in .mp4, .mkv, .webm, or .mp3.

Legal Note

Downloading videos is generally against YouTube's Terms of Service unless you own the video, have permission from the owner, or it falls under "Fair Use" (for commentary, news reporting, etc.). Always respect copyright laws and support creators by watching their content on the official platform when possible.
