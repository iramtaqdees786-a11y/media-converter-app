Why Remove EXIF Data from Your Photos?

Every digital photograph contains hidden metadata (EXIF) that can reveal sensitive information about you. This includes the exact GPS coordinates where the photo was taken, the date and time, and the specific camera or phone model used. Sharing these images on social media or forums without cleaning them first can compromise your privacy and security.

How our Metadata Removal Laboratory works?

Our Privacy Stripper tool uses a forensic-grade scrubbing algorithm to clone your image without copying any of the metadata tags. The resulting image is identical in visual quality but completely blank in terms of hidden data. We support JPG, JPEG, PNG, and TIFF formats.

Is the cleaning process secure?

Security is built into our core. All processing happens in volatile memory and is never written to permanent disk storage. Your original file is purged the instant the clean version is generated. We don't track your location, your device, or your identity.
