The Ultimate Guide to Online PDF Compression

Reducing the size of a PDF document is crucial for digital efficiency. Whether you're applying for a job, submitting a government form, or sending a large report via email, PDF compression ensures your file arrives safely without being blocked by server limits. At ConvertRocket, our laboratory uses advanced GhostScript algorithms to strip redundant data, downsample high-res images, and subset fonts, resulting in significantly smaller file sizes.

How our PDF Laboratory works?

Traditional "online compressors" often ruin the quality of your images or break the formatting of your text. Our tool offers four levels of intelligence: Screen for the smallest possible size (72dpi), eBook for a perfect balance of quality and size (150dpi), and Printer/Prepress for professional-grade results where quality is paramount. Every stream in your PDF is analyzed and optimized for its specific content type.

Is PDF Shrinking Secure?

Security is our laboratory's primary directive. When you upload a file to our secure zone, it is encrypted via TLS 1.3 during transit. The compression occurs on isolated, temporary storage that is purged the moment your session ends or 60 minutes after inactivity. We never store personal data, passwords, or the content of your documents.
