Does converting HEIC to JPG reduce quality?

Our converter preserves maximum image quality. We use advanced algorithms to maintain resolution, color accuracy, and detail during conversion. The output JPG will look identical to your original HEIC image.

Is it safe to convert HEIC files online?

Yes, completely safe. Files are processed securely with SSL encryption and automatically deleted after conversion. We never store, share, or access your images. Your privacy is our priority.

Can I convert multiple HEIC files at once?

Yes, our batch converter supports multiple HEIC files simultaneously. Upload and convert all your Apple photos in one go to save time.

Why are iPhone photos in HEIC format?

Apple uses HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) to save storage space. HEIC files are about 50% smaller than JPG while maintaining similar quality. However, HEIC isn't universally compatible, which is why conversion is often needed.

Will EXIF data be preserved after conversion?

Yes, our converter preserves EXIF metadata including date taken, camera settings, and location data (if present). This ensures your photo information remains intact.

What's the maximum file size I can convert?

Do I need to install any software?

No installation required. Our converter works entirely in your web browser. Simply visit this page, upload your HEIC file, and download the converted JPG.
