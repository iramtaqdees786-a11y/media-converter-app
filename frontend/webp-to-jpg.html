Frequently Asked Questions

What is WebP format?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior compression. However, it's not universally supported, which is why conversion to JPG is often needed.

Does WebP to JPG reduce quality?

Our converter preserves maximum image quality during conversion. The output JPG will look identical to your original WebP image.

Can I convert animated WebP?

Yes, but JPG doesn't support animation. We'll convert the first frame of animated WebP to JPG.
