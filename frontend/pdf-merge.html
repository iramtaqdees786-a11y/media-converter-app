How to Merge PDFs Online for Free

ConvertRocket provides the simplest way to combine PDF files. Whether you are merging invoices, reports, or ebook chapters, our tool ensures your files are joined safely and correctly.

  1. Click Select PDF Files and upload the documents you want to merge.
  2. Verify your files in the list.
  3. Click Merge PDFs Now.
  4. Download your single merged PDF file instantly.
