Professional PDF Grayscale Conversion

Converting a color PDF to black and white is essential for professional printing, archiving, and reducing file size. Our PDF Grayscale tool utilizes the industry-standard GhostScript engine to recalculate every pixel and vector stream into precise 8-bit gray values.

Why use 8-bit Gray Mapping?

Unlike simple color desaturation, our laboratory uses weighted luminosity calculations to ensure that text remains sharp and images retain their contrast even without color information. This process often results in a 20-40% reduction in file size, making it perfect for distribution.

Is the conversion permanent?

Once you convert your PDF to grayscale, the original color information is stripped from the document to ensure the smallest possible size. We recommend keeping a backup of your original color file if you need to revert in the future.
