The Science of Pixel Conversion

Why WebP and HEIC?

Modern formats like WebP (developed by Google) and HEIC (standardized by Apple) offer significantly better compression than the aging JPG format. However, they aren't always compatible with older software or specific social platforms. Our converter allows you to bridge that gap instantly. We use Bicubic Resampling and Neural Enhancements to ensure that when you convert "up" or "across" formats, you're getting the best possible visual result.
